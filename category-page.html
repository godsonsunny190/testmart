Cable and antenna analyzer

Cable analyzers verify the electrical connections in a cable or other wired assembly. Cable analyzers typically consist of a source of electric current, a volt meter and a switching matrix. The cable analyzer may also have a microcontroller along with a display that automates the process and shows testing results. An antenna analyzer is used for measuring the efficiency of antenna systems in radio electronics applications. In amateur radio, an antenna analyzer troubleshoots antennas and feedlines and focuses on improving the performance.
